Privacy Policy

nhatprints.etsy Self-Hosted Postiz Pinterest Automation

Effective date: March 12, 2026

This privacy policy applies to a self-hosted instance of Postiz used to schedule and publish content to Pinterest. This service is intended for limited personal or internal operational use.

Information processed

  • Pinterest OAuth credentials and access tokens needed to connect an account.
  • Pin content submitted for scheduling or publishing, including titles, descriptions, links, images, and board selections.
  • Basic operational metadata such as scheduling times, publishing status, and error logs.

How information is used

  • To authenticate with Pinterest.
  • To schedule, publish, and manage Pinterest posts on the authorized account's behalf.
  • To maintain, troubleshoot, and secure the self-hosted service.

Storage and retention

Data is stored on infrastructure controlled by the operator of this self-hosted service, which may include a local desktop or private server. Data is retained only as long as necessary to operate the publishing workflow and maintain basic records.

Data sharing

Information is not sold. Information is shared only with Pinterest and service providers strictly necessary to publish requested content or operate the self-hosted environment.

Access revocation and deletion

Access can be revoked by disconnecting the Pinterest account from the application, revoking the app from Pinterest account settings, and removing local application data from the self-hosted environment.

Security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect stored credentials and content. No method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be completely secure.
